## Taizoon Khorakiwala

![Taizoon Khorakiwala](../public/assets/references/taizoon%20khorakiwala.png)

**Taizoon Khorakiwala** — SwitzGroup

Hashim is a great architect and a wonderful human being. I’ve known him professionally since I first met him nearly a decade ago in Hyderabad and his approach to design was always creative at the same time functional. We’ve worked on a 20-acre development in Hyderabad and my apartment in Mumbai. This ability to combine beauty, cost and functionality is what sets him apart from the others. His ability to lay out a big picture as well as smaller details of the project is valuable for quality conscious clients. Having worked in diverse environments like the US, Middle East and India he has a unique understanding of culture which is so integral to design. I would have no hesitation in recommending him for a project of any size anywhere and to be paid top fees, as every penny would be worth several times over in the work he will produce and execute.
