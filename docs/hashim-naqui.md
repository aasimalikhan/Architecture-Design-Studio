# Hashim Naqui — NCD International

Biography of the company owner for NCD International.

## Credentials and affiliations

- Registered as an architect in the United States since **1986**; currently registered in several U.S. states.
- **NCARB** member since **1986**.
- Registered with the **Council of Architects, India** since **1978**.
- **Fellow of the Indian Institute of Architects**.

## Education

- **1978** — Graduated **valedictorian**, Osmania University, Hyderabad, India.
- **1988** — Master’s degree in Architecture, **Kansas State University**.

## Career path

Hashim began his career on projects in **India**, then moved to **Dammam, Saudi Arabia** in **1979**, and to the **United States** in **1982**.

### Early U.S. experience

- Projects for the **U.S. Army Corps of Engineers** in **Kansas**.
- Later work in **Texas**, the **Washington, D.C. metro area**, and **North Carolina**.

### Texas

- **Robotics Research Building**, University of Texas.
- High-rise office buildings, apartment complexes, and institutional buildings in and around **Dallas**.

### Washington metro area (from 1986)

- Large mercantile and related work: shopping centers, large grocery stores, medical facilities, office buildings, schools, and parking garages.

### North Carolina (from 1994)

- Broad project types including **RDU International Airport, Terminal 2**; laboratories; bio-tech research facilities; pharmaceutical buildings; schools; community centers; and office buildings.

### NCD International and collaboration (from 1998)

- Founded his own firm in **1998**.
- Since then, has collaborated with larger firms such as **O’Brien/Atkins** and **SfL+a** in the Raleigh area to provide production work from **India**.

### India, Middle East, and other international work

- Independent projects in **India** and the **Middle East**, including:
  - Longest building in **Doha** (**BARWA Development**) with German architects and contractors.
  - Large apartment complex **Garden Housing** under the **Agha Khan Foundation**, India.
- Large hospital projects in **India**.
- Design for a project in **Bangladesh** with **Massachusetts Institute of Technology General Hospital**, Boston.

### Current role (2022 onward)

- Associated with **SfL+a** (Raleigh & Fayetteville), assisting on state-of-the-art **school projects** in North Carolina, residential developments, and other facilities.
- Works with a team of architects based in **Hyderabad, India** since **2022**.

## Personal status

- **Citizen of the United States**.
- **Overseas Citizen of India** (OCI).
